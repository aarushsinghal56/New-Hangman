The Goal

One player (the Word Setter) picks a secret word. The other player (the Guesser) tries to guess it, letter by letter. You only have 6 wrong guesses before the hangman is complete!

How to Guess

All vowels (A, E, I, O, U) are revealed for you. Use the on-screen keyboard to guess one consonant at a time. Correct guesses fill in the blanks. Wrong guesses build the hangman.

Play Hot-Seat (2 Players)

Pass the device between players. One person enters a secret word, then hands it to their friend to guess. Perfect for playing with someone in the same room.

Play vs. Bot (1 Player)

Play against the computer! Choose a category (like Movies or Food) and a difficulty. The bot will pick a word for you to guess. Good luck!

Create a game room and send the code to a friend. You'll take turns setting words and guessing. Set a match length (like "Best of 3") and compete for the high score!
